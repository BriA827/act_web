Musicals

Into the Woods

Cinderella's Step-Mother, the wicked woman she is, was truly channeled through Bri Auspland. Being their first ever perfomance, they made an impression on the audience and the directors.

Beauty and the Beast

Playing the flamboyent Lumiere, Bri Auspland truly channeled the flame of Lumiere's spirit while bantering with Cogsworth.

Shrek

"Oh, it's you, what a surprise!" Bri Auspland would say to the crowd when playing Lord Farquaad. While they may have been lacking in size, they were not lacking in talent.

Bye Bye Birdie
