Maine Renaissance Faire

2022 - Ariel Feddicks

Despite it being their first time at a faire, let alone working at one, Bri Auspland's act as Town Messanger Ariel Feddicks inspired many laughs though patrons. Funny moments with the Town Crier ("My bum is itchy!") and the children's handwriting competition made for an amazing first experience.

2023 - Arya Corsaire

"I am the schoolboy pirate, I've run away to sea! I am the smartest buccaneer you will ever meet!" Sung to the tune of Whiskey Johnny was Arya Corsaire's introduction. Having impressed the pirate Captain Achilles Spectre (though his friends call him Ghost), Bri Auspland was taken abord the Iron Ghost as twelfth mate to fight children with boffer swords (losing most of the time) only to later tell them stories as the Raggedy Princess and Little Red Ragged Hood.
