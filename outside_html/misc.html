Miscellaneous Opportunities

2nd & 3rd Annual Pirate Invasion

Hosted at the Trails End Taphouse and Garden Center in Greenwood Lake, New York, on August 5th, the beer garden was taken over by pirates! Arya Corsaire, played by Bri Auspland, would have to break up the sword fight between the captains, only to later help train some young sailors in the ways of true pirating.
